Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my air conditioner?

It’s recommended to have your AC system serviced at least once a year to maintain optimal performance and efficiency.

What’s the best air conditioning unit for my home?

The best unit depends on factors such as room size, energy efficiency, and cooling capacity. Our team can help you choose the perfect fit.

Why is my AC not cooling properly?

Common reasons include clogged filters, low refrigerant levels, or issues with the compressor. A professional inspection can diagnose the problem.

How can I reduce my energy bill with AC usage?

Regular maintenance, using energy-efficient models, and setting the thermostat to an optimal temperature (around 24°C) can help lower energy costs.
